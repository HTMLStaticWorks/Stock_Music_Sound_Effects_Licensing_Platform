Frequently Asked Questions

What does Royalty-Free mean?

Royalty-Free means you only pay once for a license and can use the music for as long as you want within the terms of that license, without paying additional ongoing fees based on views or plays.

Can I use the music on YouTube?

Yes. If you have a Commercial license or higher, you can monetize your YouTube videos. We will automatically clear any Content ID claims on your registered channel.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

Any projects you published while your subscription was active remain permanently cleared. However, you cannot use downloaded tracks in new projects after your subscription ends.

Do you offer stems?

Yes, stems (isolated instruments) are included with Commercial, Broadcast, and Film/Gaming licenses, as well as premium subscription tiers.
