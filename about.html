About AudioSync

Empowering creators with world-class audio.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate musicians and filmmakers, AudioSync was born out of frustration with complex, restrictive music licensing. We wanted to build a platform where creators could easily find high-quality tracks and use them freely without worrying about copyright strikes or hidden fees.

Today, AudioSync connects thousands of independent artists with millions of video creators, agencies, and game developers worldwide.

Our Mission

To democratize access to premium audio, ensuring every creator, regardless of budget, can elevate their storytelling with professional sound.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted and comprehensive creative marketplace for audio assets, fostering a sustainable ecosystem for independent artists.
