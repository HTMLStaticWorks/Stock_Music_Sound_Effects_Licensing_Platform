Pricing FAQ

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time from your dashboard settings. Upgrades take effect immediately.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! We offer a 50% discount for eligible students and educators. Please contact support with your .edu email.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. For Enterprise plans, we can arrange wire transfers.

Can I use the music for commercial client work?

Yes! Our Pro Creator and higher plans fully cover commercial work for your clients without any additional licensing fees.

Are there any daily download limits?

No. All of our subscription plans come with unlimited downloads so you can focus on creating without boundaries.
